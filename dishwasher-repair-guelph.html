Dishwasher Repair Costs & Common Issues

A service call in Guelph runs $80–$120, applied toward the repair. Drain pump replacement, one of the most common dishwasher repairs, typically runs around $180. Door latch or hinge repair runs $120–$180 and is usually a quick fix — most dishwashers won't start at all unless the safety latch fully engages. Control panel repair for unresponsive buttons or dead displays runs $150–$280, and spray arm or drain hose issues run $100–$180.

Poor cleaning performance is almost always a spray arm or pump issue rather than a detergent problem — check that spray arm holes aren't clogged with hard water deposits or food debris before assuming a mechanical failure. Drainage problems are frequently caused by a clogged filter basket at the bottom of the tub, which is a simple homeowner-cleanable part worth checking before booking a service call.

Dishwashers typically last about 10 years. If the repair is under 50% of a comparable new unit's cost (typically $500–$1,000 installed) and your dishwasher is under 5 years old, repair is usually the better value — and simple fixes like spray arms, latches, and inlet valves are worth doing at almost any age.
