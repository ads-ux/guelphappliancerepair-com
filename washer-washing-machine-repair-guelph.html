Washer Repair Costs & Common Issues

A service call in Guelph runs $80–$120, applied toward the repair. Won't-drain or pump failure is the most common washer repair, running $150–$280. Lid switch or door latch issues, a simple and inexpensive fix, run $120–$220. Excessive vibration from worn drum bearings runs $250–$400 — labour-intensive, especially on front-loaders where disassembly takes longer. Control board failure runs $250–$450 including parts, and leaks run $120–$250 depending on whether the source is a door seal, pump seal, or hose connection.

Front-load washers are the most common repair call in Guelph. They're generally efficient machines, but door boot seals, bearings, and pump filters are known weak points over time. Mold around the door seal is usually a maintenance issue — leaving the door open between cycles prevents it — rather than something requiring repair.
