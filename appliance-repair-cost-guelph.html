How Much Does Appliance Repair Cost in Guelph?

Published 2026-06-01 — Guelph Appliance Repair

Appliance repair pricing in Guelph follows a consistent structure, though the final cost depends on the appliance, the fault, and parts availability. Here's what to expect in 2026.

Service Call / Diagnostic Fee

Most Guelph appliance repair companies charge a $80–$120 service call fee that covers the visit, diagnosis, and repair quote. Reputable companies apply this fee toward the cost of the repair if you proceed. Some charge a flat diagnostic fee; others roll it into the labour rate.

Labour Rates

Hourly labour in Guelph runs $90–$140/hour for appliance repair technicians. Most common repairs take 30–90 minutes once the technician is on site with the right parts. Repairs that require ordering parts and a second visit add another service call fee in some cases — ask upfront.

Repair Costs by Appliance

  • Refrigerator: $150–$400. Common repairs: thermostat ($150–$200), compressor ($300–$600+, often not worth it on older units), ice maker ($200–$350).
  • Washer: $150–$350. Common repairs: pump ($150–$250), lid switch or door latch ($120–$200), control board ($200–$400).
  • Dryer: $100–$250. Dryers are simpler — heating element ($120–$180), thermal fuse ($100–$150), belt ($100–$160).
  • Dishwasher: $150–$300. Common repairs: pump motor ($180–$280), door latch ($120–$170), control board ($200–$350).
  • Oven/Stove: $120–$350. Bake element ($120–$180), igniter ($150–$250), control board ($250–$400).

When to Repair vs. Replace

The general rule is: if the repair cost exceeds 50% of the replacement cost of a comparable new appliance, replacement is usually better value. Practical thresholds for Guelph:

  • Fridge over 12 years old with a compressor or sealed system issue → replace
  • Washer over 10 years old with a drum bearing or control board failure → replace
  • Dryer over 12 years old with a simple belt or element → still worth repairing
  • Dishwasher over 8 years with a pump motor failure → borderline, worth a quote
